Welcome to Marist Parking App!


Effortlessly navigate campus parking with our intuitive mobile application designed specifically for the Marist College community. Say goodbye to circling lots endlessly and hello to stress-free parking!

    Features:
  • Real-time availability: Know exactly where parking spots are available on campus, saving you time and frustration. Personalized notifications: Receive alerts when your favorite lots have available spaces or when your parking session is about to expire.

  • Easy payment: Pay for parking directly through the app, no need to fumble for loose change or visit a pay station.

  • Campus map integration: Seamlessly integrate with Marist's campus map to help you find your way to available parking spots. Experience convenience like never before with Marist Parking App.
